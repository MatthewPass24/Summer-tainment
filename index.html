Grounds for Sculpture

When my cousins stayed with us over the summer, we went to Grounds for Sculpture. This park contained impressive statues, sculptures, and other art pieces. It shined through its natural beauty and was a unique attraction to see.

Seaglass Hunting

Sea glass is glass that's worn down from rocks and waves for years, leaving it smooth and frosted. These pieces are collectable and can be entered into competitions depending on rarity. Over the summer, I found my first sea marble!

Six Flags

I went to Six Flags a couple of times with my friends and met up with my cousins in Jackson too. We rode most of the roller coasters, such as Nitro and Medeusa. I also went to Hurricane Harbor one time for the water slides.
